American Airlines flies to 269 domestic destinations and 92 international destinations in 48 countries (as of March 2023[update]). The list includes the city, country, and the airport's name, with the airline's hubs, seasonal destinations, and previously served destinations marked where applicable. This list does not include airports served by only American Eagle, the airline's regional hub transport brand.
